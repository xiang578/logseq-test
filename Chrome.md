[[快捷键]]

黑暗模式控制
关闭 `defaults write com.google.Chrome NSRequiresAquaSystemAppearance -bool YES`
打开 `defaults write com.google.Chrome NSRequiresAquaSystemAppearance -bool NO`

修改插件的快健键 `chrome://extensions/shortcuts`
